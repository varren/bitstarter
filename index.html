All Tomorrow's Parties is a young indie rock band based in Moscow, Russia. Their sound is a fusion of garage rock and psychedelic rock.

Vadim Tikhonov

Vocals, guitars

ATP gathered in summer 2011 in Tashkent, Uzbekistan and soon the band began to gain underground popularuty performing in clubs, pubs and rock oriented venues of their hometown.

Ruslan Tikhonov

bass guitar

In 2012 All Tomorrow's Parties took the first place in the rock bands contest organized by local promo Iosis Fest - Become a and won the right to record a single in Iosis Studio.

The group is currently unsigned because rock music in their country remains an underground phenomenon.

On March 29, 2013 All Tomorrow's Parties took part in Ilkhom Rock Festival as a headlining act and performed a conceptual gig on the scene of Ilkhom Theater.

From Nov 2012 to May 2013 All s Parties released 6 digital demo singles through the web.

In July, 2013 the band moved to Moscow, Russia and released their first demo album recorded in Tashkent.
